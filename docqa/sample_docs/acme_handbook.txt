Acme Cloud — Support Handbook

Password reset
To reset your password, click "Forgot password" on the login page. You will
receive an email with a reset link that is valid for 30 minutes. If the link
expires, request a new one. For security, password resets are not available
over phone or chat.

Two-factor authentication
Two-factor authentication (2FA) can be enabled under Settings > Security. We
support authenticator apps (TOTP) and hardware security keys. SMS-based 2FA is
being deprecated and will be removed at the end of the year.

Refunds and billing
Annual plans are billed yearly and include a 14-day free trial. Refunds are
available within 30 days of the initial purchase. To request a refund, contact
support with your order number. Monthly plans are non-refundable but can be
cancelled at any time, stopping the next charge.

Mobile apps
The Acme mobile app is available for iOS and Android. Search "Acme Cloud" in the
App Store or Google Play. The mobile app supports offline mode for documents
that have been opened at least once while online.

Data residency
Customer data is stored in the region selected at signup: US, EU, or APAC. Data
does not leave its region except for aggregated, anonymized usage metrics.
Enterprise customers can request a signed data processing agreement.

Service limits
Free accounts are limited to 5 projects and 1 GB of storage. Pro accounts allow
unlimited projects and 100 GB of storage. Rate limits apply to the API: 60
requests per minute on Free, 600 on Pro, and custom limits on Enterprise.
